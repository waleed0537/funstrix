Have Questions?

Common Questions About Getting Started

What technology do I need?

Just a computer, tablet, or smartphone with internet connection and Zoom installed. We'll help you set everything up!

How long are the classes?

Each class is 45 minutes long - the perfect duration to keep kids engaged and learning without getting tired.

Can I change class times?

Yes! We offer multiple time slots throughout the week. You can switch to a different time that works better for your family.

What if my child misses a class?

No worries! We provide catch-up materials and your teacher will help your child stay on track with the curriculum.
