    Frequently Asked Questions

    Quick Answers to Common Questions

    How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

    We respond to all inquiries within 2 hours during business hours (Mon-Fri 9AM-6PM AEST). For urgent matters, please call us directly.

    Can I schedule a call to discuss my child's needs?

    Absolutely! We offer free consultation calls where our education experts can discuss your child's specific learning needs and recommend the best program.

    Do you offer free trial classes?

    Yes! We offer free trial classes for both our Writers Club and Readers Club programs. This allows your child to experience our teaching style before committing.

    What if I'm in a different time zone?

    While our programs are designed for Australian time zones, we have students from around the world. We'll work with you to find suitable class times.
