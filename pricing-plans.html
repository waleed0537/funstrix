    Pricing Questions & Answers

    Everything you need to know about our pricing

    Is there a registration fee?

    Yes, there's a one-time registration fee of $80 that covers all administrative costs, learning materials, and platform access for the entire duration of your enrollment.

    Can I switch plans during the term?

    Yes! You can upgrade your plan at any time during the term. We'll calculate the price difference and you can pay the remaining amount to access additional features.

    What happens if my child misses a class?

    All classes are recorded and available for 48 hours after each session. Additionally, we provide make-up sessions for students who miss more than 2 classes per term.

    Do you offer family discounts?

    Yes! Families with multiple children receive a 15% discount on the second child and 20% on the third child. Contact us for more details about our family packages.

    Is the 30-day guarantee really no questions asked?

    Absolutely! We believe in our programs 100%. If you're not completely satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll provide a full refund. Your child's success and your satisfaction are our top priorities.
