    Our Mission

    Transforming Education Through Fun

    At Funstrix, we believe every child deserves access to quality education that inspires and engages. Our mission is to revolutionize online learning by making it fun, interactive, and accessible to all Australian children and beyond.


    We're committed to nurturing young minds through innovative teaching methods that combine the Australian curriculum with creative, game-based learning experiences. By fostering a love for reading and writing, we're building confident learners who are prepared for academic success and beyond.

    Our Core Values

    These values guide everything we do at Funstrix

    Fun First

    Learning should never be boring! We infuse every lesson with games, activities, and engaging content that makes students excited to learn.

    Excellence in Education

    We maintain the highest standards in curriculum development and teacher training, ensuring every student receives top-quality education.

    Our Journey

    The Funstrix Story

    2020

    The Beginning

    Funstrix was born during the global pandemic when Ambreen recognized the urgent need for quality online education. Starting with just 10 students, we launched our Writers Club to help children continue learning from home.

    2021

    Rapid Growth

    Our community grew to over 500 students. We expanded our team of teachers and launched the Readers Club to meet growing demand for comprehensive literacy education.

    2022

    Going Global

    Funstrix expanded internationally, welcoming students from New Zealand, UK, South Korea, and the Middle East. We reached 2,000+ active students and established our signature teaching methodology.

    Today

    Leading the Way

    With over 5,000 students and a team of 30+ dedicated teachers, Funstrix is Australia's favorite online writing and reading program. We continue to innovate and expand, always staying true to our mission of making learning fun.
